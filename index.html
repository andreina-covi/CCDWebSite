About CCD Bolivia

El Club de Ciencia de Datos es una comunidad apasionada por la ciencia de datos, que busca la aplicacion practica en su campo de experiencia, asi tambien como el aprendizaje tanto para el desarrollo personal o para dar soluciones a problemas del entorno social.

Nuestra Vision es llegar a ser una comunidad que se dedica a impartir conocimiento e investigar sobre la ciencia de datos y sus ramas afines.

Ph.D. in Computer Science

Alexander Bergel

Alexandre Bergel is Associate Professor and researcher at the University of Chile. Alexandre Bergel and his collaborators carry out research in software engineering. His focus is on designing tools and methodologies to improve the overall performance and internal quality of software systems, by employing profiling, visualization, and artificial intelligence techniques.

He has authored over 120 articles, published in international and peer reviewed scientific forums, including the most competitive conferences and journals in the field of software engineering. Alexandre has participated to over 120 program committees of international events. Alexandre has also a strong interest in applying his research results to industry. Several of his research prototypes have been turned into products and adopted by major companies in the semi-conductor industry and certification of critical software systems. Alexandre authored the book "Agile Visualization", the book "Deep Into Pharo". His new book, titled "Practical Agile AI with Pharo", will be released mid 2020.

Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering

Julio Godoy

Julio Godoy is Assistant Professor at University of Concepcion.

His research interests are: Multi-agent learning and planning, Multi-robot navigation, Machine learning, Data science, Game theory, Distributed sensor networks, Analysis of algorithms.

Julio has authored articles in journals, conferences and workshops. Furthermore, he has had different presentations in international conferences, workshops and seminars.

His current research focuses on developing machine learning algorithms to ensure safe and efficient motion for multi agent navigation, when moving in crowded environments.
